### Issue #25 — cascade-break defaults research note (commit `7f096c1`)
Worktree-isolated agent wrote `dev/research/cascade-break.md` (392
lines) deriving (or not deriving) the defaults `ratio = 0.5` and
`eps = 1e-10` from the published literature.

**Conclusion: empirical, not derivable.** `ratio = 0.5` was
calibrated on `pinene_3200_0009` in #8 and cross-validated against
the bimodal `n_delayed_in / expanded_ncol` distribution measured in
#15. Wächter & Biegler 2006 uses `κ⁻_w = 1/3` not `1/2`;
Schenk & Gärtner 2006 has no delay-fraction predicate;
Bunch & Kaufman 1977's `α ≈ 0.6404` is unrelated. `eps = 1e-10`
sits in the published range used by LAPACK / MA57 / PARDISO but no
single source yields the exact value. Recommendation: keep the
May-2026 posture (defaults at `None, None`, opt-in only); keep
`0.5 / 1e-10` as the recommended opt-in pair; rely on
`bench_issue8` to lock the values to the calibration matrix.

References added to `dev/references.bib`: `schenk2006fastfact` (ETNA
2006), `higham2002accuracy` (SIAM 2002, 2e). Both flagged as "no
local PDF" in the note's References section.

No `src/` changes.
