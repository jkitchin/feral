  135.7 s), confirming the cascade is structural to the matrix.

## Track A2 investigation (this session, after the checkpoint)

The A2 investigation continued past the checkpoint. It first reframed
the fix as "the 2×2 stability gate" — then **delay-cause
instrumentation refuted that over-read** and pinned the real
mechanism. Full diagnosis:
`dev/research/kkt-cascade-amplifier-2026-05-21.md`.

- **`CB=1` on pinene → 100× speedup, 493.9 s → 4.86 s** (iter 6-9
  fronts from 64-208 s to ~45 ms). Cascade-break *does* break this
  cascade — but **iter 9 returns `WrongInertia`** (got 63999/63995/1,
  want .../0): the `PerturbToEps` floor 1e-10 sits below the inertia
  zero-tolerance, so a force-accepted near-zero pivot still
  classifies as zero on the most-converged iterate. Inadmissible —
  the *same* failure issue #46 recorded for `allow_delayed_pivots
  =false`. CB stays a fallback only, not the production fix.
- Added scalar-path delay-cause `panel_diag` counters and re-ran
  `probe_issue46_supernode pinene_3200_0009.mtx`. The cascade is an
  **amplifier × two triggers**:
  - **Amplifier — break-on-first-delay.** The BK driver loop
    (`factor.rs:1719-1849`) does `Delayed => break` then
    `n_delayed = ncol - nelim` (1848-1849): one delayed pivot
    forfeits the whole remaining tail of the supernode. 3936 delay
    events → `n_delayed = 133648` (~34 columns/event). Config 2
    (static pivoting, `n_delayed=0`, healthy 1.25× factor) proves
    the forfeited columns are pivotable — the forfeit throws away
    real work.
  - **Trigger A — split MC64 pairs (2840 events,
    `scalar_1x1_delay`).** 1×1 delays whose matched partner is not
    co-located (`a[k][k+1]==0` → `partner=None` → last-resort 1×1).
    Matches the 3781 split-across-supernodes pairs — the residual
    #46 co-location gap.
  - **Trigger B — growth-bound rejection (1096 events:
    `scalar_2x2_delay_growth=1059`, `…both=37`).** Co-located saddle
    2×2 candidates that failed the Duff-Reid growth bound (816 with
    `det<0`, genuine indefinite saddles). The SSIDS det floor fires
    **0** times — not involved.
- **Correction to the prior framing.** The text above (and the
  earlier journal/plan) said A2 is "fix the 2×2 stability gate" and
  pointed at `fallback_2x2_need_swap_or_bound=31542`. That was an
  over-read: `need_swap_or_bound` is the *panel delegating* a
  swap-2×2 case to `scalar_pivot_step`, not a rejection. The true
  delay split is 2840 (1×1) / 1096 (2×2 growth) / 0 (det floor).
