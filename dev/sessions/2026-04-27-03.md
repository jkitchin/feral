## Decisions Made
- **Wrapper pattern for in-place kernel.** Rather than break the public
  `factor_frontal_blocked(&SymmetricMatrix, ...)` API and force tests
  + examples + the bit-parity reference to all switch to `&mut`,
  introduce `factor_frontal_blocked_in_place(&mut SymmetricMatrix, ...)`
  alongside it and migrate only the hot-path callers in the
  multifrontal driver. Tests keep the simpler immutable API; the
  driver pays no copy.
- **W-3a alone does not meet the 8 ms CHAINWOO target.** It is a
  prerequisite for W-3b (output Vec pooling) and W-1 (engage panel for
  smaller `ncol`) which together close the gap to MUMPS. Land W-3a as
  a clean isolated commit; the larger speedups follow.
