## Decisions Made

- **F2.2 acceptance reframed** (also in `dev/decisions.md`): drop
  the `[0.5, 5.0]` gate against MUMPS RINFOG(11). The two
  estimators measure different quantities and direct ratio
  comparison is meaningless. Replacement: harness exists end-to-
  end; F2.1 Hilbert/KKT calibration remains the binding numerical
  gate. Future work option recorded in plan: build a real 1-norm
  oracle by extending `mumps_bench.F` with solve(A, e_i) sweeps
  on a smaller calibration set.

- **F3.2b scoped to single-supernode Schur tail** (carried from
  session 09 journal F3.2b entry). Multi-supernode tail returns
  `InvalidInput` and is deferred to F3.3. Adequate for the IPM
  reduced-system use case where the Schur block is small (~tens
  to hundreds of variables) and falls naturally into one root
  supernode under AMD-on-non-Schur + Adjacency amalgamation.

- **ICNTL(11)=1 not =2** in `mumps_bench.F`. The plan note
  specified `=2` but per `dsol_driver.F:5742`, `=2` is the
  cheaper variant that skips LCOND and leaves RINFOG(9..11)
  unset. Followed the plan's *intent* (cross-validate condition
  estimates) over its *letter*. Documented in
  `mumps_bench.F` and the F2.2 commit message.
