# Session 2026-05-12-02

## Goal

Investigate cont-201's residual ~30%-of-ceiling parallel headroom
(prior session measured 1.44× speedup at T=8 vs theoretical 4.83×).
Two hypotheses to falsify before fixing anything: (a) mutex
contention on the shared `contrib_blocks` store, (b) rayon task-spawn
overhead at 11121 tasks.

## Accomplished

1. **Added `AtomicLockStats` opt-in telemetry to `NumericParams`**
   (`src/numeric/factorize.rs`). Six wait/hold/body/task counters at
   the three global mutex sites, plus eight per-phase wall-time
   counters wrapping the sequential prologue/epilogue inside
   `factorize_multifrontal_supernodal_parallel`. Default `None`; no
   perf impact when disabled.

2. **Extended `solver_parallel_lock_breakdown` test** to run a
   cold+cached factor pair, reset all 14 atomics between, and report
   stats from the second (cached-symbolic) call so the breakdown
   reflects the pounce/IPM production hot path.

3. **Falsified the mutex-contention hypothesis.** Worst-case (cont-201)
   total mutex wait+hold = 4.8 ms of 123.5 ms aggregate body across 4
   workers — 3.9%. c-big sits at 0.02%. A lock-free contribution
   store would buy ≤4% on the worst matrix and is not worth chasing.

4. **Identified what cont-201's "30% of ceiling" cold-shot wall
   actually was**: ~157 ms of `symbolic_factorize` in
   `Solver::factor` on cache miss. The parallel driver's own work is
   ~54 ms total (loop + non_loop). Once the `Solver` pattern-cache is
   hit (the pounce/IPM regime), cont-201 wall drops **214 → 56 ms,
   a 3.8× cached-mode improvement**.

5. **Quantified the real cached-mode headroom for cont-201**: 1.5×
   inside rayon::scope itself, not at lock sites.
   - non_loop driver overhead = 9.0 ms / 56.2 ms = 16% of wall
   - rayon::scope = 45.1 ms; body_per_T = 30.9 ms; **loop
     utilization 68.5%**
   - critical-path 23.5 ms + non_loop 9.0 ms = 32 ms ideal at T=4
   - measured 56.2 ms / ideal 32 ms = **1.5× residual headroom**

6. **Wrote up findings**: `dev/debugging/2026-05-12-cont201-cached-headroom.md`.

## Decisions Made

- **Do NOT redesign the contribution-block store to be lock-free.**
  Telemetry falsifies the contention hypothesis (≤4% of body time on
  the worst matrix; ≤0.04% on c-big). Recorded in
  `dev/decisions.md`.

- **Keep `AtomicLockStats` as a permanent opt-in diagnostic surface.**
  Default `None`; zero runtime cost when not wired. Test stays
  `#[ignore]`'d.

## Abandoned Approaches

- **Lock-free contribution-block store** — investigated by
  instrumentation, found the lock is not the bottleneck. See
  `dev/tried-and-rejected.md`.

- **Attributing cont-201's residual headroom to rayon task-spawn
  overhead** — the parallel driver only spawns rayon tasks once per
  supernode; even at 11121 tasks the lock-wait + body data shows the
  loop itself (work-stealing tail, dep-chain idle inside the
  rayon::scope) is the limit, not task creation.
