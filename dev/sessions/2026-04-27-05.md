## Decisions Made

- **Pivot from corpus-first-then-decide to AMF-now**. User pushback:
  MUMPS uses HAMF4 as the default for SYM=2 N≤10000, the
  AMD-vs-AMF distinction is well-established for indefinite
  problems, and bordered/arrowhead patterns (ORBIT2-class) are not
  rare in optimization workloads. Decision recorded in the journal
  pivot entry; full rationale in `dev/research/amf-clean-room.md`
  §1.
- **Architectural pattern for AMF**: extract shared quotient-graph
  machinery behind a `Metric` trait in `feral-ordering-core` (or a
  sibling crate); `feral-amd` and `feral-amf` become thin
  specializations with monomorphized inner loops. Rationale in
  `dev/plans/amf-clean-room.md` §A. Will be ratified by Phase A
  outcome — append to `dev/decisions.md` once Phase A lands.
- **Test oracle is nnz_L within 10% of MUMPS HAMF4**. Byte-exact
  permutation match is infeasible (tie-breaking depends on
  linked-list insertion order). Recorded in
  `dev/research/amf-clean-room.md` §9.
