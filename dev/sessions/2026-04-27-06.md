## Decisions Made

- **`feral-ordering-core` is the home for shared quotient-graph
  internals, not a new sibling crate.** Considered breaking out a
  dedicated `feral-quotient-graph` crate; rejected because
  CONTRACT_VERSION already covers the contract types and adding a
  third crate would over-scope the dependency graph. The shared
  internals live in a `quotient_graph::` submodule with explicit
  internal-but-pub commentary.
- **`AmdOptions::aggressive` is not workspace state** and stays a
  parameter on `run_elimination`. Only `dense_alpha` participates
  in workspace construction; `aggressive` is a per-iteration
  policy. This keeps `WorkspaceOptions` minimal.
