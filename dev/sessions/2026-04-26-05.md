expanded-corpus bench. Determine whether they are numerical failures

  inertia against the MUMPS-derived sidecar for any family. Reports

  2. Bulk sign disagreement: SSCOSINE_0000 reports 935 of 5000
     expected-positive pivots as negative. Factorization solves
     the system tight; only the inertia labels differ.
