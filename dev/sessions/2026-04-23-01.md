## Decisions Made

- **Rook pivoting will be implemented as a rescue path, not a top-level
  strategy.** Splice inside `try_reject_1x1_frontal` so it fires only
  when BK-partial fails the column-relative threshold. Zero cost on
  well-conditioned matrices (the 99% case); full rescue benefit on
  ill-conditioned KKTs. No user-facing flag; no dispatch policy.
  Matches HSL MA57 behavior, which is what Ipopt consumers expect.
- **Blocked BK will not be upgraded to a real BLAS-3 GEMM right now.**
  Phase 2.4.1 perf target is met (p90 ≤ 2.0). The bit-parity guarantee
  of the deferred-axpy design is a debugging asset worth keeping until
  a specific perf regression demands otherwise.

Both decisions appended to `dev/decisions.md`.
