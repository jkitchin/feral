## Accomplished

**Diagnosed #46 — and overturned the three-agent research diagnosis.**
The session opened with a three-agent research phase (feral source,
MUMPS 5.8.2, SPRAL SSIDS) that converged on "analysis-phase ordering
failure, fix = broaden `pick_ordering_preprocess`'s activation
predicate". Two ground-truth probes on the real CHO KKT refuted every
load-bearing claim:

- `probe_issue46_preprocess` — feral stores only the lower triangle, so
  KKT constraint columns are stored-degree 0/1, *not* high-degree. The
  existing `low_degree(≤2)` predicate already fires (frac 0.7505);
  `LdltCompress` already activates on the CHO KKT. Activation is a no-op
  fix.
- `probe_issue46_supernode` — with `LdltCompress` active: symbolic
  `factor_nnz_estimate` 1.22M, numeric `factor_nnz` 28.05M (23× blowup),
  max supernode ncol 133 (no giant root), **96.6 % of MC64 pairs
  co-located adjacent**. The cascade is a purely numeric delayed-pivot
  blowup, not an ordering failure.

Root cause: the numeric Bunch-Kaufman kernel re-derives pivots blind
via magnitude-argmax and never consults the analysis-phase MC64
pairing. For a zero-diagonal constraint column whose largest coupling
points out-of-front, it delays — even though the co-located saddle
partner sits at the adjacent column.

**Fixed it** (`070840b`). `scalar_pivot_step` (`src/dense/factor.rs`)
2×2 partner selection is now two-tier: argmax row `r` when `r` is fully
summed, else the next column `k+1` when `k`/`k+1` are coupled
(`a[k,k+1] != 0`). The 2×2 stays gated by the Duff–Reid growth bound +
SSIDS det floor — widens the search, not the acceptance. ~30-line diff,
bit-identical to the prior kernel whenever `r` is fully summed.

Evidence — CHO `parmest` KKT, production path: factor 11.7 s → 0.20 s
(57×), factor-nnz 28.05M → 3.35M, inertia (21672,21660,0) unchanged.
feral now ~3× MA57 (was ~160×).

**Committed regression test** `tests/issue_46_saddle_kkt_cascade.rs`
(in `070840b`). Synthetic zero-(2,2)-block saddle KKT, n=500,
constraints-first layout, `Identity` scaling (isolates the kernel).
Verified against a temporarily-reverted kernel: pre-fix cascades to a
61× fill blowup (n_delayed=398, n_2x2=0) → test fails; fixed kernel
holds 0.83× (n_delayed=0, n_2x2=199) → test passes. Oracles: inertia
(nv,nc,0) by the saddle-point inertia theorem; solve residual identity
≤ 1e-8; factor-nnz ≤ 5× the symbolic estimate.

**Committed the diagnostic probes** (`eb77966`) as reproducible tooling.

Verification: `cargo test --release` all green (0 failures);
