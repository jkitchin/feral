## Decisions Made

Appended to `dev/decisions.md` 2026-04-17:

1. **Index width is `i32`.** `CscPattern` borrows `&[i32]`;
   permutations are `Vec<i32>`. Matches Ipopt / MUMPS / SSIDS
   convention.
2. **No trait, one free function per crate.** Each ordering crate
   exposes `fn {name}_order_full(...)`. Crate-specific options and
   stats stay per-crate; only `CscPattern`, `OrderingStats`, and
   `OrderingError` cross the boundary.
3. **No elimination tree in the contract.** Etree / symbolic
   factor live downstream in the analysis phase; node-separator
   backends (METIS/SCOTCH/KaHIP) do not synthesize a fake etree.
