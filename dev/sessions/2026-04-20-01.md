## Decisions Made

- **D.4 thresholds finalized:** `N_TINY = 16`, unconditional
  (density-independent). Chosen to cover all observed top-10
  tiny-n rows (max n = 11) with comfortable margin and to match
  the research-note recommendation. Appended to
  `dev/decisions.md`.

- **The 80× HS85_0022 "regression" in the D.3 stage-3 bench was
  single-shot noise.** Diagnosed definitively — full pipeline is
  37 µs p50 under 50 cold reps. This is a recurring pattern: the
  bench harness's per-matrix single-shot timing at the tens-of-µs
  scale produces 10–80× noise excursions. Future top-N worst-ratio
  rows should be treated as candidates-for-probe, not as confirmed
  regressions. Multi-run variance (geomean stable, max swings
  11→286×) confirms the mode.

- **D.4 closed after stage 2, no stage-3 follow-up.** The unique
  D.4 class (`n ≤ 16 AND ρ < 0.25`) is empty or near-empty on the
  current IPM corpus. The predicate change is correct and the
  stage-1 probe confirms the per-call speedup when such matrices
  appear, but there is no population of target matrices to justify
  further work (stack buffer, workspace pooling for dense scratch)
  at this time.
