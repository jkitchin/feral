## Decisions Made

- **Phase D ships as opt-in only this session.** Adding the AMF
  enum variant and dispatch arm is independent of and lower-risk
  than flipping `pick_default_method`. The default flip needs
  the Phase C corpus regen completed first so per-cluster fill
  ratios can be validated before turning a new ordering loose on
  hundreds of thousands of in-tree matrices. This decision keeps
  the autonomous-loop commit small and lets the corpus regen be
  a deliberate user-initiated step.
