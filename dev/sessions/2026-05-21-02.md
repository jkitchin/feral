### Cascade broken on pinene_3200

`probe_issue46_supernode pinene_3200_0009.mtx` (n=127995, 63995 MC64
pairs), before vs after Fix 1:

| metric      | before Fix 1 | after Fix 1 |
|-------------|-------------:|------------:|
| n_delayed   |       133648 |       11309 |
| factor_nnz  |      ~165.7M |    3 601 077 |
| fill blowup |          69× |       1.51× |
| factor time |       ~183 s |        78 ms |
| inertia     | (64000,63995,0) | (64000,63995,0) |

Inertia **exact and unchanged** — 64000 + 63995 = 127995 = n, zero
count 0; saddle-point inertia theorem (Benzi/Golub/Liesen 2005 §3.4)
satisfied. ~2350× factor speedup. The residual 11309 delays are the
genuine (un-amplified) count from Triggers A/B in the research note;
at 1.51× blowup / 78 ms this is well inside budget — **Fix 2/3 are
not needed**.

The static no-delay config gives a smaller blowup (1.25×) but the
*wrong* inertia (64001,63994,0) — force-accept misclassifies one
eigenvalue. Fix 1's delayed path is both fast and inertia-exact;
static pivoting is not.
