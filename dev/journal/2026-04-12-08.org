  Step 4 complete. Added the Duff-Reid 2×2 growth bound inline at
  both factor_frontal and factor 2×2 acceptance sites. Computes
  RMAX, TMAX by scanning rows k+2..nrow of columns k, k+1, with
  AMAX = |d21|. On rejection, falls back to a single 1×1 at k;

  Third surprise: my first Test D (u_accept=0.01, u_reject=0.1)

  0.12*u, so the rejection equality was u* = 0.708 — far above
  0.1. Had to bump u_reject to 0.9 to cross the threshold. The
  hand-trace:
    k=0: col 0 gamma0=0.3 at row 2, akk=0.1, BK 1×1 fails.
    r=2, gamma_r=0.3, arr=0.05, swap-1×1 fails.
    LAPACK: 0.1*0.3=0.03 < 0.6404*0.09=0.0576 → fails.
    2×2 with r=2, swap positions {1,2}. New 2×2 block:
      d11=0.1, d21=0.3, d22=0.05, |det|=0.085
    RMAX=0.3, TMAX=0.3, AMAX=0.3.
    LHS2 = (0.1*0.3 + 0.3*0.3)*u = 0.12*u > 0.085 iff u > 0.708.
