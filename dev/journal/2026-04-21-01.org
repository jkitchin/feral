* 06:25 :parallel:race:root-cause:
Divergence hit at attempt 68. Diff of sorted hash streams:
: 84d83
: < FRONTAL_HASH snode=173 nrow=177 ncol=27 hash=3a222...
: 164a164
: > FRONTAL_HASH snode=9 nrow=5 ncol=2 hash=b7237...
Run A: 174 hashes including snode 9 once and snode 173 once
(root). Run B: 174 hashes including snode 9 TWICE and snode
173 ZERO times. This is a task-graph dispatch bug, not a
dense-kernel nondeterminism bug. A supernode is being factored
twice and another is being skipped.

Traced the dispatch: factorize_multifrontal_supernodal_parallel
seeds leaves via ~pending[i].load() == 0~ inside rayon::scope.
While the seed loop runs on the caller, workers execute
spawned leaves and decrement parents' pending counters. Any
non-leaf whose final child completes during seeding becomes
pending==0 and gets spawned by the caller — AND the last
child's trampoline via ~fetch_sub==1~ also spawns it. Double
dispatch.
