* 23:45 :issue-13:phase-c:scope:
Restarted Phase C the same evening. Field lifecycle analysis: =l=,
=d_diag=, =d_subdiag=, =perm=, =perm_inv= all move into long-lived
=NodeFactors= so they have no recycle point during factorisation.

the parent's =extend_add= and dropped. That is the natural recycle
target. Pooling the long-lived fields would need an ABI break or a
=take_into_factors= swap; deferred indefinitely.
