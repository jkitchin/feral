before the loop; extract solve_sparse_core_into taking caller-owned
y_buf and w_buf via a private SolveWorkspace struct; reuse the
workspace across the (up to) 11 internal solves; update x in place
rather than constructing x_new each step; drop the separate ax/r_new
buffers (compute A*x directly into r and negate-add). Tests pass.
