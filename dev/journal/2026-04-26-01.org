Iterative refinement is the deciding factor; ordering is
irrelevant. The 4–5 orders-of-magnitude jump from plain to
refined says the factor itself is fine — the plain triangular
back-substitution is just sitting on the conditioning edge of
=n·eps·1e6=. Conclusion: =diag_mittelmann.rs= should use
=solve_sparse_refined=, which is what a production IPM call site
calls anyway.
