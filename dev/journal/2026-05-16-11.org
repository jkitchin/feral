- worst per-matrix slow +50.00% (criterion <= +5%)  FAIL

DECISION: REJECT.  Default stays =SmallLeafBatch::Off=.

The +50% / +25% / +10% outliers on n=5, 6, 12 matrices are
1 µs timer noise (2→3 µs, 4→5 µs, etc.) — the only signal in
tiny is rounding error.  Small-bucket median is +1.9%, within
the ±5% per-matrix noise band the Phase 2.11 5-run repeat
already documented.  Real signal (large bucket +7.7% median)
is out of scope for issue #11 — the criterion is tiny+small.

The hypothesis (SIMD + APP would expose driver-overhead
savings) is not supported: the kernel-cost reduction did not
shift the gate flip out of noise on the target bucket.
