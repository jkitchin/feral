* 21:30 :phase-2.5.1:step2:diagnosis:
  Re-examined CSparse cs_counts.c control flow. Three corrections:
    (a) I had partner/current variable roles swapped — the
        condition is `first[current] > maxfirst[partner]` and
        we need `partner > current` (lower-triangle rows).
    (b) I was missing the head-of-loop `delta[parent[j]] -= 1`
        for every non-root j — this cancels the double-count
        introduced by the accumulation merge at the end.
    (c) Delta increments target the CURRENT postorder node
        (column j in CSparse terms), not the partner row.
