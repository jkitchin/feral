Per-supernode alloc count is 17-23 across every matrix size - a
structural fingerprint. Every supernode allocates ~20 small Vecs
(row_indices, row_map=~vec![usize::MAX; n]~, frontal
SymmetricMatrix, FrontalFactors internals, contrib_row_indices).

Cost attribution on AVION2_0000: at ~50ns/alloc + 30ns/dealloc
that is (1125×50)+(692×30) ≈ 77µs of 99µs budget. The remaining
~20µs is plausibly the floating-point work - which is roughly
MUMPS's 19µs. MUMPS gets there by reusing one big workspace
sliced by metadata, not by allocating per front.
